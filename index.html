CS Tutoring Center


Welcome to the CS Tutoring Center, which provides FREE peer-to-peer support for all students in lower division computer science courses. All tutoring sessions will be done remotely and via Zoom this semester. Please check out the Calendar below.

Announcements

We use the USFCS 'general' channel on the USF CS slack group to make important announcements. For instance, since technical interruptions, while unlikely, are inevitable, if you join a Zoom session and we are not there, it is because of such unforeseen Internet outage. In such rare events, we will post an announcement to the USFCS 'general' channel on the USF CS slack group.

Events and Workshops

All events and workshops hosted by our CS department (listed below) are FREE for any student to attend. However, some events may require RSVP due to limited space. Your suggestions for events and workshops are welcome, and may be sent to the USFCS 'general' channel on the USF CS slack group.
