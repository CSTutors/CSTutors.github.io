Fall Social 2013

Join us on Tuesday, October 29, 2013 from 7:00pm to 9:00pm for our first annual fall social! There will be challenges, prizes, and free food!

Challenges

The CS Tutoring Center is hosting a series of challenges to celebrate the opening of the new Lo Schiavo Center for Science and Innovation. The first 10 students to complete all of the challenges will win a prize!

The first challenge will kick off in CSI G12 at 7:00pm, and there will be at least one challenge per floor at CSI. You do not need to complete the challenges in-order, but it is recommended. See below for details:

To participate in a challenge, find the challenge monitor in the specified room. S/he will have additional details for each challenge. For example, to participate in the programming challenge, visit CSI G12 and talk to TBA.

Some challenges will test your programming skills, while others will be photo scavenger hunts. For the photo challenges, you will need the Flickr App installed on your smart phone.

Prizes

The first 10 students to complete all of the challenges will get to choose from the following prizes:

These include: Light Mine Mini, Chewbacca Star Wars Plush, LEGO Mini Storage Box, Credit Card Lightbulb x2, Pac-Man Stress Toy, 8-Bit Sunglasses, Space Invaders Fridge Magnets, AT-AT Star Wars Plush Vehicle, Tetris Stress Blocks, Floppy Disk Drink Coasters, and a Large No Comment T-Shirt.

Everyone that participates in one or more of the challenges are invited to join us in CSI 307 for free food and drinks!

Social

Students and faculty are invited to join us in CSI 307 for free food and drinks, and to check out the photos submitted for the challenges. You do not need to participate in the challenges to join us! Stop by CSI 307 anytime between 7:00pm and 9:00pm.
