Faculty Sponsors

  • Vahab Pournaghshband

    Dr. Pournaghshband is the founder and director of Networks & Security Research Laboratory (Nets Lab). His research is the areas of Computer Networks and Security. He is committed to promoting a diverse and inclusive learning environment in computer science, and consistently develops pedagogies to incorporate cybersecurity into all computer science courses. Dr. Pournaghshband is the recipient of Fulbright Scholar Award in Austria. Vahab received his BS and MS from UC Berkeley. He received his PhD in Computer Science from UCLA, and most recently was a professor at California State University, Northridge, before joining USF in 2018.
