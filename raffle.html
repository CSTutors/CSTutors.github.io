Eligibility

All USF students that use the CS Tutoring Center are eligible for the raffle. Just remember to signin before receiving help at the Tutoring Center:

Sign In Now!

You are entered in the raffle if you signin at least once to the CS Tutoring Center. While you are encouraged to signin to the CS Tutoring Center every visit, multiple entires in the signin form will not affect your chances of winning.

Prior to CS Night, we will collect all of the unique USF usernames from the signin form and select one of those usernames randomly. We will announce the winner at CS Night on Thursday, December 5, 2013, and contact that winner using his or her @dons.usfca.edu email account.
