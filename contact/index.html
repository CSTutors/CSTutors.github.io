Appointments

If none of the scheduled tutoring sessions work with your schedule or if you feel you need extra help, please feel free to email tutoringcenter@cs.usfca.edu and schedule an individual tutoring appointment. Please remember that our tutors are also students, so there may be limited availability before exams.

Faculty Sponsor

The faculty sponsor meets weekly with the tutors, provides mentoring, and helps coordinate CS Tutoring Center services. The current faculty sponsor is Professor Sophie Engle. Please email sjengle@cs.usfca.edu to contact the faculty sponsor directly.
